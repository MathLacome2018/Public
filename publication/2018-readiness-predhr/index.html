Monitoring players' readiness using predicted Heart Rate responses to football drills

M. Lacome, B.M. Simpson, N. Broad, M. Buchheit

Abstract

GPS and HR data were analysed during typical small-sided games and a 4-min standardized submaximal run (12 km/h).
