Biography

I’m a Strength & Conditioning coach who turned into an applied sports scientist by passion for sciences & innovation. I have over 10 years of experience in elite team sports, in rugby union and now in football. I mainly work on training load, performance & fatigue monitoring. My areas of interest are data analysis, visualisation and all things around innovation.
