Welcome

Aug 27, 2018 2 min read

Have you ever collected data with your team and wonder what you can do with them? How to get insights from them and how to communicate clearly your results?

… You’re not alone! I asked myself this question millions of times over the last 10 years.

I will try to post here on a regular basis. I will try to write the posts I would have love to read years before. My idea is simple: I want to show what we can do with some data in term of analysis or visualisation to get better insight on how players cope with training load. Is their performance improving or are they struggling to cope with the load?

Daily, I mainly use 3 tools: Excel, R & Tableau software. I’m an expert in none of them but I will try to give some advice on how to use these tools to delve into the data and bring some insights to the coaching staff. As I told you – I’m not a data scientist, nor a data viz specialist and not even an native English speaker! So, please, when you are reading my post, if you see any way to improve my analyses, visualisation or just the flow of my words, tell me & I will adapt!

Propose me new ways of thinking, improved R code as well as nicer or simply more efficient data visualisation. I really think that the power of crowd and thus, open innovation, is way better for creativity & innovation than what can do 1 brain, even if it was the best brain in the world.

Mathieu.
